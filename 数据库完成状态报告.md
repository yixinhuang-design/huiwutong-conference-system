### 查看用户
```sql
SELECT tenant_id, COUNT(*) as user_count FROM sys_user GROUP BY tenant_id;
-- 结果：
-- 租户 1: 7 用户
-- 租户 2: 3 用户
-- 租户 3: 3 用户（如已执行扩展脚本）
-- 租户 4: 4 用户
-- 租户 5: 1 用户
```

### 查看权限结构
```sql
-- 查看租户 1 的权限树
SELECT 
  u.username,
  r.role_name,
  p.permission_name
FROM sys_user u
JOIN sys_user_role ur ON u.id = ur.user_id
JOIN sys_role r ON ur.role_id = r.id
LEFT JOIN sys_role_permission rp ON r.id = rp.role_id
LEFT JOIN sys_permission p ON rp.permission_id = p.id
WHERE u.tenant_id = 1
ORDER BY u.username, r.role_name, p.permission_name;
```
